Signal mapping

Layout Builder


In the Signal Mapping dashboard all the signals that are needed for the simulation and program can be defined. The signals are communications between the manufacturing resources to initiate a certain interaction at a certain moment.


At the top of the dashboard the controller has to be selected for which the signals are listed below. A Connected to filter can be applied to show all the signals or just for a selected resource that is connected to the controller. While scrolling through the filter list, the corresponding component in the 3D View is cross-highlighted.
