Edit support plate crossing

 

At creation of the fixture, the top shape of the support plates is matching with the contour of the workpiece that it supports. However, for special fixation of the workpiece the crossing of two support plates can be given an additional design feature, a pin or a pin hole.

The Change type command opens a sub level to select the new type of the crossing. The design update is executed automatically.
