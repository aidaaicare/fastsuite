When the Create command is started, the system automatically activates the Manipulator in the 3D View. The Manipulator now drives the nesting design.

The UV plane of the Manipulator is the reference plane for the nesting sheets, with the center as starting corner point. The U-axis of the Manipulator is the reference for the orientation of the nesting plate.

When the Manipulator has been positioned accordingly, the Pie menu can be called from the center.
