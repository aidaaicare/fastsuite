Resize support plate

Edit support plate

 

With the Re-size command the support plate length can be modified.

 

When started, the Manipulator appears at the nearest side of the support plate from where the command has been initiated. At that side the length of the plate can be modified by simply dragging the Manipulator, while the other side remains fixed. After release the length of the support plate is recomputed.

 

Resize

Result

The command remains active after a re-size operation. It can be terminated by closing the Manipulator or by pressing the key.

Dragging the Manipulator outside the base plate boundary will re-size the support plate to its maximum possible length on the base plate.
