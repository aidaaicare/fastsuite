Lock adapter

Edit layout adapter


The Lock command is a switch to lock or unlock the adapter for any (accidental) manipulation.


After locking the adapter, it is marked with a red color.

Mechanical

Electrical

A locked adapter cannot be connected to any other adapter. A locked socket adapter or a locked active plug adapter prevents the component from being moved in the layout.

Applying the command on the wire of an electrical connection will lock the adapters at both sides of the connection.

A locked connection cannot be separated.
